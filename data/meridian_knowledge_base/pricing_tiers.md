# Pricing Plans & Feature Comparison

## Overview

Meridian AI offers three pricing tiers designed to meet the needs of teams of all sizes. All plans include access to the core Meridian Analytics Platform (MAP) with varying levels of features, support, and scale.

## Starter Plan

- **Price:** $49/user/month (minimum 1 user, maximum 10 users)
- **Data Source Connectors:** 5 connectors
- **Row Limit:** 10 million rows
- **Dashboards:** Up to 10 dashboards
- **Scheduled Reports:** Up to 10 scheduled reports
- **Support:** Community support only
- **SSO:** Not available
- **SLA:** No uptime SLA

The Starter plan is ideal for small teams getting started with analytics. It includes the essential features needed to connect data, build dashboards, and generate insights.

## Professional Plan

- **Price:** $149/user/month (minimum 5 users, no maximum)
- **Data Source Connectors:** Unlimited connectors
- **Row Limit:** 1 billion rows
- **Dashboards:** Unlimited dashboards
- **Scheduled Reports:** Up to 50 scheduled reports
- **Support:** Email + chat support
- **SSO:** Included
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime SLA

The Professional plan is designed for growing teams that need more data capacity, advanced features like SSO, and responsive support.

## Enterprise Plan

- **Price:** Custom pricing (minimum 25 users)
- **Data Source Connectors:** Unlimited connectors
- **Row Limit:** Unlimited rows
- **Dashboards:** Unlimited dashboards
- **Scheduled Reports:** Unlimited scheduled reports
- **Support:** Dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM), phone support
- **SSO:** Included
- **HIPAA BAA:** Available
- **SLA:** 99.95% uptime SLA

The Enterprise plan is built for large organizations that require unlimited scale, dedicated support, and compliance certifications.

## Add-On Modules

### Advanced ML Add-On
- **Price:** $500/month flat fee
- **Availability:** Professional and Enterprise tiers only
- Includes automated ML model training, feature engineering, and prediction endpoints.

### Data Governance Add-On
- **Price:** $300/month (Professional), included in Enterprise
- Column-level access controls, data lineage, PII auto-detection, and audit logging.

### Real-Time Streaming Add-On
- **Price:** $750/month
- **Availability:** Professional and Enterprise tiers only
- Supports Kafka, Kinesis, and Pub/Sub streaming sources.

## Annual Billing Discount

All plans are eligible for an annual billing discount of 20% off the monthly price when billed annually. Contact sales for Enterprise annual pricing.

## Free Trial

We offer a free 14-day trial of the Professional tier. No credit card required to start.
