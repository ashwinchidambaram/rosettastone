# Billing FAQ -- Invoices, Upgrades & Cancellations

## Overview

This document covers frequently asked questions about billing, invoicing, plan changes, and account management for Meridian AI.

## Invoicing

Invoices are generated on the 1st of each month. Payment is due within 30 days of invoice generation (NET-30 terms). You can view and download invoices from Settings > Billing > Invoices.

## Payment Methods

- **Credit card:** Accepted on all tiers (Starter, Professional, and Enterprise).
- **ACH / Wire transfer:** Available on the Enterprise tier only.

Payment information can be updated at any time in Settings > Billing > Payment Methods.

## Plan Upgrades

Mid-cycle upgrades are prorated. When you upgrade your plan in the middle of a billing cycle, you are charged only for the remaining days of the cycle at the new plan rate. The upgrade takes effect immediately.

For example, if you upgrade from Starter to Professional 15 days into a 30-day billing cycle, you are charged 50% of the Professional rate for the remainder of the cycle.

## Plan Downgrades

Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. If you downgrade your plan, you retain access to your current plan's features until the end of the current billing period. The lower rate begins on the next billing cycle.

Note: If your usage exceeds the limits of the lower tier (e.g., row count, number of connectors), you will need to reduce your usage before the downgrade takes effect.

## Cancellation

- **Monthly plans:** Can be cancelled at any time. Access continues until the end of the current billing period.
- **Annual plans:** Require a 30-day notice for cancellation. Contact your account manager or email billing@meridian-ai.com to initiate cancellation.

Upon cancellation, your data is retained for 90 days. After 90 days, all data is permanently deleted.

## Overage Charges

If your workspace exceeds its plan's row limit, overage charges apply:

- **Rate:** $0.10 per 1,000 rows over the plan limit
- **Billing:** Overage charges are billed monthly in arrears (charged on the following month's invoice)

You will receive an email warning when your usage reaches 80% of the plan limit.
