# Remote Work & Office Attendance Policy

## Overview

Meridian AI is a "remote-first" company. We believe great work can happen anywhere, and we design our processes and tools to support distributed teams. This document outlines our remote work and office attendance policies.

## Remote Work

There are no mandatory in-office days for most roles at Meridian AI. Employees can work fully remote from anywhere in the continental United States. Certain roles with specific on-site requirements (e.g., facilities, hardware engineering) may have different arrangements.

## Office Attendance (Hybrid Roles)

Employees based in Austin, NYC, and London are expected in-office 2 days per week -- specifically Tuesday and Thursday. These in-office days are designed for team collaboration, meetings, and social connection. Other days are flexible.

Employees not based near an office location are fully remote and are not expected to travel to an office regularly.

## Home Office Stipend

Meridian AI provides a home office stipend to help employees create a productive work environment:

- **New hire stipend:** $1,500 one-time payment for setting up your home office (desk, chair, monitor, etc.)
- **Annual stipend:** $500/year ongoing for maintenance, upgrades, and office supplies

The stipend is paid as a reimbursement. Submit receipts via Workday Expenses.

## International Remote Work

International remote work is allowed for up to 30 days per year, subject to manager and Legal approval. This policy covers situations like working while traveling abroad or visiting family in another country.

To request international remote work:
1. Submit a request in Workday at least 2 weeks in advance.
2. Include the country, dates, and duration.
3. Your manager and the Legal team will review for tax and compliance implications.
4. Once approved, you may work from the international location.

Extended international remote work (beyond 30 days) requires VP-level approval and may have tax implications.

## Core Collaboration Hours

To facilitate collaboration across time zones, Meridian AI observes core collaboration hours: 10 AM to 3 PM in each employee's local time zone. During core hours, employees should be available for meetings, Slack messages, and real-time collaboration. Outside of core hours, work is flexible and asynchronous communication is expected.
