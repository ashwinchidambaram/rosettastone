# Meridian Analytics Platform -- Product Overview

## About Meridian AI

Meridian AI is a B2B SaaS company founded in 2021, headquartered in Austin, TX. We serve mid-market and enterprise organizations across retail, healthcare, financial services, and manufacturing with our AI-powered data analytics platform.

## Meridian Analytics Platform (MAP)

The Meridian Analytics Platform (MAP) is our core product. MAP ingests structured and unstructured data from customer systems, applies ML-driven transformations, and surfaces insights through customizable dashboards, automated reports, and a natural-language query interface called AskMeridian.

MAP supports 40+ data source connectors out of the box, including Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, PostgreSQL, Salesforce, HubSpot, and many more. Whether your data lives in cloud data warehouses, CRMs, or flat files, MAP connects seamlessly.

## AskMeridian Natural Language Query

AskMeridian is our natural language query (NLQ) feature that lets users ask questions in plain language. AskMeridian NLQ feature supports English, Spanish, French, German, and Japanese, making it accessible to global teams.

## Scale and Performance

MAP processes up to 10 billion rows per workspace on the Enterprise tier, ensuring even the largest datasets are handled efficiently. The platform was launched in 2022, and version 4.0 was released in January 2025 with significant performance improvements and new features.

## Pricing Tiers

Meridian AI offers three pricing tiers -- Starter, Professional, and Enterprise -- with add-on modules for Advanced ML, Data Governance, and Real-Time Streaming. Each tier is designed to meet different organizational needs, from small teams exploring analytics to large enterprises requiring dedicated support and compliance certifications.

## Reliability

We stand behind our platform with industry-leading SLAs. The uptime SLA is 99.95% for Enterprise, 99.9% for Professional, and there is no SLA for the Starter tier. Our infrastructure is designed for high availability with automated failover and redundancy across multiple availability zones.

## Compliance

Meridian AI maintains SOC 2 Type II and HIPAA BAA compliance, making the platform suitable for regulated industries including healthcare and financial services. See our Security and Compliance documentation for details.
