# Paid Time Off & Leave Policy

## Overview

Meridian AI offers a comprehensive time-off policy designed to support work-life balance for all employees. This document outlines PTO, holidays, sick leave, parental leave, and bereavement leave policies.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Full-time employees receive 20 days of PTO per year, plus 10 company holidays, plus 5 sick days per year. PTO accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month. PTO is available for use as it accrues -- you do not need to wait until the full annual allotment is available.

### PTO Rollover

Unused PTO rolls over to the following year, up to a maximum of 10 days. Any PTO beyond 10 days that is unused at the end of the calendar year will be forfeited. We encourage employees to take their PTO throughout the year.

### Requesting PTO

PTO requests are submitted via Workday, our HR management system. Manager approval is required for requests of more than 3 consecutive days. Requests for 3 days or fewer are automatically approved unless there is a scheduling conflict.

Please submit PTO requests at least 2 weeks in advance for planned time off.

## Company Holidays

Meridian AI observes 10 company holidays per year. The holiday schedule is published in January for the upcoming year and is available in Workday.

## Sick Leave

Each employee receives 5 sick days per year. Sick days do not accrue and do not roll over. Sick leave can be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an immediate family member who is ill.

## Parental Leave

Meridian AI provides generous parental leave:

- **Primary caregiver:** 16 weeks paid leave
- **Secondary caregiver:** 8 weeks paid leave

Parental leave is available for birth, adoption, or foster placement. Leave must begin within 12 months of the qualifying event. Parental leave can be taken consecutively or in blocks with manager approval.

## Bereavement Leave

Bereavement leave is available for employees who experience the loss of a loved one:

- **Immediate family** (spouse/partner, parent, child, sibling): 5 days paid leave
- **Extended family** (grandparent, aunt, uncle, cousin, in-law): 3 days paid leave

Additional unpaid leave may be granted at manager discretion for extended bereavement needs.

## Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees receive PTO, sick leave, and parental leave on a prorated basis relative to their scheduled hours.
