# Getting Started -- First 30 Minutes

## Overview

Welcome to Meridian AI! This guide will walk you through setting up your workspace, connecting your first data source, and building your first dashboard in under 30 minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Workspace

Workspace creation takes less than 2 minutes. To get started:

1. Go to [app.meridian-ai.com/signup](https://app.meridian-ai.com/signup).
2. Enter your company email address. Note: free email providers (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.) are not accepted -- a company domain is required.
3. Verify your email address via the confirmation link.
4. Choose your workspace name and region.

Your workspace will be provisioned immediately after email verification.

## Step 2: Start Your Free Trial

Meridian AI offers a free 14-day trial of the Professional tier. No credit card is required to start the trial. You will have full access to Professional features including unlimited data source connectors, SSO, and up to 1 billion rows.

## Step 3: Connect Your First Data Source

The first data source connection wizard supports one-click OAuth for Snowflake, BigQuery, and Salesforce. For other data sources, you can enter connection credentials manually.

To connect a data source:
1. Navigate to Settings > Data Sources > Add New.
2. Select your data source type.
3. Follow the connection wizard -- for Snowflake, BigQuery, and Salesforce, click "Connect with OAuth" for a seamless setup.
4. Once connected, Meridian will automatically discover your schema and begin an initial sync.

## Step 4: Explore the Sample Dataset

Every new workspace comes pre-loaded with the "Meridian Demo Retail" sample dataset. This dataset includes sample retail data with customers, orders, products, and revenue metrics. You can use it to explore MAP features before connecting your own data.

## Step 5: Build Your First Dashboard

1. Navigate to Dashboards > Create New.
2. Select the data source (your connected source or the Meridian Demo Retail dataset).
3. Drag and drop widgets onto the canvas.
4. Configure each widget with the metrics and dimensions you want to visualize.
5. Save and share your dashboard with your team.

## Step 6: Invite Your Team

Go to Settings > Team > Invite Members. Enter email addresses for your colleagues and assign them roles (Viewer, Editor, or Admin). They will receive an email invitation to join your workspace.

## Onboarding Checklist

The recommended onboarding flow is:
1. Create workspace
2. Connect data source
3. Build first dashboard
4. Invite team

Following this checklist ensures you get the most value from Meridian as quickly as possible.
